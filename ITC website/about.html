About Institute Technical Council

The Institute Technical Council (ITC) is the central hub for all tech-related activities at IIT Bombay. We strive to encourage innovation, tinkering, and hands-on experimentation through a variety of clubs, workshops, and projects.

Our mission is to foster an environment where students can pursue new ideas, learn from peers and mentors, and ultimately shape the future of technology. Whether you’re an experienced programmer or just starting out, ITC offers something for everyone!

Get in touch with us to learn more about our initiatives, or explore the various technical teams under the ITC umbrella. You’ll find a place to belong, collaborate, and create.
